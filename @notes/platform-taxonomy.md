# Platform Taxonomy: Domains and Specialties

This document defines the two-tier hierarchical taxonomy used to categorize all entities on the RetireAway platform, including Specialists, Service Offerings, and Resources.

## Taxonomy Overview

| Domain (Parent) | Specialties (Children) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Immigration** | Visas, Residency, Citizenship, Golden-Visa, Immigration-Law |
| **Finance** | Tax, Banking, Currency-Exchange, Wealth-Management, Pensions |
| **Healthcare** | Health-Insurance, Clinical-Care, Elder-Care, Medical-Tourism, Wellness |
| **Housing** | Purchasing, Rentals, Property-Management, Real-Estate-Law |
| **Logistics** | Household-Shipping, Pet-Relocation, Vehicle-Importation, Customs |
| **Integration** | Utilities-Setup, Language-Translation, Driver-Licenses, Education, Community |
| **Work** | Remote-Work, Freelancing, Corporate-Setup, Local-Employment, Co-working |
| **Lifestyle** | Recreation, Travel, Dining, Hobbies, Volunteering |

---

## Domain Definitions

### 1. Immigration
*   **Definition:** The legal mechanisms of entering, residing, and securing citizenship in a destination country.
*   **Strategic Intent:** Addresses the user's legal right to stay.

### 2. Finance
*   **Definition:** The management, movement, protection, and taxation of wealth.
*   **Strategic Intent:** Addresses capital preservation and cross-border financial compliance.

### 3. Healthcare
*   **Definition:** Medical services, health coverage, and clinical well-being.
*   **Strategic Intent:** Addresses physical risk mitigation and access to care.

### 4. Housing
*   **Definition:** The acquisition, management, or leasing of a physical residence.
*   **Strategic Intent:** Addresses the user's primary physical domicile.

### 5. Logistics
*   **Definition:** The physical execution of moving people, pets, and property across international borders.
*   **Strategic Intent:** Addresses the mechanical challenges of relocation.

### 6. Integration
*   **Definition:** The practical, cultural, and bureaucratic tasks required to function day-to-day after arriving.
*   **Strategic Intent:** Addresses immediate structural assimilation and local compliance.

### 7. Work
*   **Definition:** The legal, structural, and practical aspects of generating active income while abroad.
*   **Strategic Intent:** Addresses location-independent work, remote employment, and entrepreneurship.

### 8. Lifestyle
*   **Definition:** The experiential, leisure, and social elements of living in the destination country.
*   **Strategic Intent:** Addresses quality of life, community, and recreational fulfillment.
