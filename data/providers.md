# Retirement Providers

This document lists organizations providing services for expats and retirees.

## Thailand

### Visa / Immigration
- **Newland Chase**
  - **Website**: [https://newlandchase.com/](https://newlandchase.com/)
  - **Contact**: +66 2 105 4032
  - **Description**: A premier global immigration firm specializing in complex residency pathways and high-level permits. They provide full-spectrum support for the Long-Term Resident (LTR) visa and the Destination Thailand Visa (DTV), catering specifically to high-net-worth retirees and professionals.
  - **Category**: Visa / Immigration

- **Thai Visa Centre**
  - **Website**: [https://thaivisacentre.com/](https://thaivisacentre.com/)
  - **Contact**: +66 2 331 4509 / +66 2 508 8428
  - **Address**: Building 91/11, Bangkok, Thailand
  - **Description**: A highly-rated agency known for transparent pricing and simplifying the bureaucracy of Thai immigration. They specialize in standard Non-O (Retirement) and Marriage visas, offering in-person assistance at immigration offices and managing 90-day reporting.
  - **Category**: Visa / Immigration

### Banking
- **Kasikorn Bank (KBank)**
  - **Website**: [https://www.kasikornbank.com/](https://www.kasikornbank.com/)
  - **Contact**: +66 2 888 8888
  - **Description**: The leading choice for tech-savvy expats due to its superior English-language mobile interface (K-PLUS app). They offer specialized services like "The Wisdom" priority banking and are generally flexible with documentation for foreigners holding long-term visas.
  - **Category**: Banking

- **Bangkok Bank (BBL)**
  - **Website**: [https://www.bangkokbank.com/](https://www.bangkokbank.com/)
  - **Contact**: +66 2 645 5555
  - **Description**: The most established bank for international transfers and the preferred choice for those receiving pensions from abroad. It features a large international branch network and specialized Foreign Currency Deposit (FCD) accounts to help manage exchange rate risks.
  - **Category**: Banking
