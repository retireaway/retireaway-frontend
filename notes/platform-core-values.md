# Platform Core Values & Goals

## The Northstar
To guide future retirees from zero awareness to a comprehensive understanding of every requirement for a successful, comfortable retirement in their destination of choice.

## Core Values

1. **End-to-End Empowerment**
   - We support the user through the entire lifecycle: from the years leading up to retirement (planning) to the period immediately following relocation (settling).
2. **Research-First Integrity**
   - Every piece of information must be synthesized from verified resources. We prioritize objective research over marketing narratives.
3. **Actionable Clarity**
   - We don't just provide information; we provide the "how-to" and the "who" (Specialists).
4. **Holistic Support**
   - We address the retiree as a whole person, covering everything from legal residency (Immigration) to hobbies and social life (Lifestyle).

## Strategic Goals

- **Zero-to-One Education:** Eliminate the "unknown unknowns" for retirees.
- **Service Discovery:** Connect users with vetted Specialists for execution.
- **Maturity Tracking:** Help users track their progress across all 8 Retirement Pillars.
