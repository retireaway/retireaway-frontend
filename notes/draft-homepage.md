# RetireAway Homepage Specification Draft

## Platform Overview

RetireAway is a retirement research platform with the goal of helping future international retirees make informed decisions on their retirement.
The platform aims to function both as a one stop shop for retirement research and a launchpad facilitating deeper research into various aspects for retirement.

This will be accomplished by;

- gathering resources and expertise from various resources on any and all topics relating to international retirement
- sythesizing that information into various formats that are easily accessible and digestible by retirees
- augmenting the synthesized information by incoperating rich links to the different resources to the relevant topics.

The core unique selling point of the platform at the moment is the RetireAway Matchmaker
In its current stay the user answers a few questions and is presented with a list of retirement country each given a retirement score
This score reflects how well said country matches the user based on the answers given

## Hero

```text
                                    [hero-text]
                    [Countries] [Specialists] [Resources]
                                    [searchbar]
                                [matchmaker-cta-card]
```

- _Hero Text_
  Large bold centered hero text introducing the platform.
  Should be short 2 to 4 words
- _Secondary Navigation_
  A short list of quick links to various dedicated pages listing out Countries, Specialist and resources
- _Searchbar_
  A global search bar to search for all content on the platform
- _Matchmaker Card_
  This is the most visually dominate element on the homepage landing screen intended to lead the user into the matchmaking questionnaire
  It is split into two columns
  The left column contains an image
  The right column contains a large bold title 6 to 10 words long
  A small subtitle intended to fit onto one or two lines
  A button
